Module 5: Examples

Summary

Concepts

Freefall is when an object moves only under the force of gravity. Although air resistance or friction usually affect falling objects, we often ignore it in calculations to simplify and still consider it freefall. Acceleration due to gravity is approximately -10 m/s².

Equations

x = x₀ + vt + ½at² | Solving for position
