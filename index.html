Leveraging Digital Distraction to Educate Students

2/3 of students get distracted by their devices in school

Digital distraction is a huge problem, and it’s only getting bigger. Generation Alpha is growing up with an unrelenting flood of digital content at their fingertips. It’s time for education to adapt to the real world.

SigmaSolutions is here to help. Instead of fighting digital distraction, we’ve decided to embrace it. Our learning modules are designed to seamlessly integrate distractions into the educational process, so students never have to face the challenge of boredom. With SigmaSolutions, you’ll learn more by doing less—equipping students with the essential life skill of consuming information without ever truly absorbing it.
